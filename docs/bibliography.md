# Bibliography

Curated references — academic and industry — supporting the lecture's content.

## Graph Theory Foundations

- Newman, M. E. J. (2010). *Networks: An Introduction*. Oxford University Press.
- Easley, D., & Kleinberg, J. (2010). *Networks, Crowds, and Markets: Reasoning About a Highly Connected World*. Cambridge University Press. [Available online](https://www.cs.cornell.edu/home/kleinber/networks-book/).

## Algorithms

- Page, L., Brin, S., Motwani, R., & Winograd, T. (1999). *The PageRank Citation Ranking: Bringing Order to the Web*. Stanford InfoLab.
- Blondel, V. D., Guillaume, J.-L., Lambiotte, R., & Lefebvre, E. (2008). *Fast unfolding of communities in large networks*. Journal of Statistical Mechanics: Theory and Experiment, 2008(10).
- Traag, V. A., Waltman, L., & van Eck, N. J. (2019). *From Louvain to Leiden: guaranteeing well-connected communities*. Scientific Reports, 9(1).

## Graph Query Languages

- ISO/IEC 39075:2024 — *Information technology — Database languages — GQL*. International Organization for Standardization. [iso.org](https://www.iso.org/standard/76120.html)
- Francis, N., et al. (2018). *Cypher: An Evolving Query Language for Property Graphs*. SIGMOD.

## Knowledge Graphs

- Hogan, A., et al. (2021). *Knowledge Graphs*. ACM Computing Surveys, 54(4).
- Noy, N., et al. (2019). *Industry-scale Knowledge Graphs: Lessons and Challenges*. Communications of the ACM, 62(8).

## Graph Neural Networks

- Sanchez-Lengeling, B., Reif, E., Pearce, A., & Wiltschko, A. B. (2021). *A Gentle Introduction to Graph Neural Networks*. Distill. [distill.pub](https://distill.pub/2021/gnn-intro/)
- Wu, Z., et al. (2021). *A Comprehensive Survey on Graph Neural Networks*. IEEE Transactions on Neural Networks and Learning Systems, 32(1).
- Bilot, T., El Madhoun, N., Al Agha, K., & Zouaoui, A. (2023). *Graph Neural Networks for Intrusion Detection: A Survey*. IEEE Access, 11.

## Cybersecurity-Specific Graph Applications

- Robbins, A., Vazarkar, R., & Schroeder, W. (2016). *Six Degrees of Domain Admin*. DEF CON 24. Foundational presentation behind BloodHound.
- Noel, S., et al. (2016). *CyGraph: Graph-Based Analytics and Visualization for Cybersecurity*. Handbook of Statistics, Vol. 35.

## Threat Intelligence and STIX

- OASIS (2021). *STIX™ Version 2.1 Specification*. OASIS Open. [oasis-open.org](https://docs.oasis-open.org/cti/stix/v2.1/stix-v2.1.html)
- Connolly, J., Davidson, M., & Schmidt, C. (2014). *The Trusted Automated eXchange of Indicator Information (TAXII)*. MITRE.

## Tools and Platforms

- Wireshark Foundation. *Wireshark User's Guide*. [wireshark.org](https://www.wireshark.org/docs/wsug_html/)
- Neo4j, Inc. *The Neo4j Graph Data Science Library Manual*. [neo4j.com/docs](https://neo4j.com/docs/graph-data-science/)
- SpecterOps. *BloodHound Community Edition Documentation*. [bloodhound.specterops.io](https://bloodhound.specterops.io/)
